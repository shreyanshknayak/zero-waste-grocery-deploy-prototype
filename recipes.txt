Spinach Fried Rice
Ingredients: leftover rice, spinach, garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil
Steps:
1. Heat sesame oil in a pan.
2. Add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
3. Add chopped spinach and cook until wilted.
4. Add leftover rice and mix well.
5. Splash soy sauce and stir-fry for 3–4 minutes.
6. Serve hot.

Carrot Spinach Stir Fry
Ingredients: carrots, spinach, olive oil, cumin seeds, salt
Steps:
1. Heat olive oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
2. Add chopped carrots and sauté until tender.
3. Add spinach and stir until wilted.
4. Add salt to taste and cook for another 2 minutes.
5. Serve as a side dish or with rice.

Leftover Rice Pancakes
Ingredients: leftover rice, chickpea flour, onion, green chili, salt, turmeric
Steps:
1. Mix all ingredients with water to make a thick batter.
2. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladle of batter.
3. Spread into a pancake and cook both sides till golden brown.
4. Serve with chutney or yogurt.

Vegetable Scrap Soup
Ingredients: carrot peels, spinach stems, onion skins, garlic, vegetable stock
Steps:
1. Wash all scraps thoroughly.
2. Boil scraps with garlic and onion in vegetable stock for 30 minutes.
3. Strain or blend depending on texture preference.
4. Season and serve warm.

Creative Veggie Wrap
Ingredients: spinach, shredded carrots, leftover cooked rice, tortilla, hummus
Steps:
1. Spread hummus on the tortilla.
2. Add rice, spinach, and carrots.
3. Wrap tightly and toast slightly if desired.
4. Slice and serve as wraps.
